Game Objectives

Select 1 to 3 cards.

PC: Select a tile above and click on an empty space to place it. Click on a tile on the board to swap it. Hover to rotate, right-click to flip, and double-click to remove.
Mobile: Tap a tile above to select, tap an empty space to place. Tap a tile on the board to rotate. Tap and hold to flip. Double-tap to remove.
